How the technology was use to be a weapon in a form of Disinformation
Disinformation, also known as "fake news," is a growing problem in today's society. It refers to the deliberate spread of false or misleading information with the intention of manipulating public opinion and causing confusion. The rise of the internet and social media has made it easier than ever for disinformation to spread quickly and reach a large audience. Disinformation can take many forms, from fabricated news stories and conspiracy theories to manipulated images and videos. It can be spread through various mediums such as social media, traditional news outlets, and even through word of mouth. The consequences of disinformation can be severe, from undermining trust in democratic institutions to inciting violence and spreading hate.
